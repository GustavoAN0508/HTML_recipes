feijoada

Ingredients:

  • •1kg of black beans
  • •70g of pig's ears
  • •70g of pig's feet
  • •50g of pig's loin too
  • •150g of portuguese sausage
  • •100g of dry steak

spicing:

  • •2 big onions cutted in little pieces
  • •3 bay leaves
  • •6 garlic cloves
  • •1 or 2 oranges
  • •salt and pepper

Let's prepare it!

  1. put every piece of steak on water for 36 hours straight
    and try to change it periodically
  2. cook the steaks, squishy ones goes first then the hard ones
  3. when every steak turn softier, put'em out
  4. finally, spice it

And there you have your feijoada!
